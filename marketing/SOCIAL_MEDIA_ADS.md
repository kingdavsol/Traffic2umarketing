# QuickSell Social Media Ads
## Facebook & Instagram Campaign

**Campaign Date**: November 2025
**Platforms**: Facebook, Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories, Reels
**Target Markets**: United States, Canada, UK
**Budget Allocation**: $50,000 initial campaign

---

## Ad Strategy

### Target Audiences

1. **Resellers & Flippers** (Age 25-55)
   - Interest: eBay, Mercari, buying/selling items
   - Behavior: Active in reselling communities
   - Income: $30K-150K+

2. **Entrepreneurs & Side Hustlers** (Age 20-45)
   - Interest: Side income, passive income
   - Behavior: Follow business/finance pages
   - Income: $40K+

3. **Small Business Owners** (Age 30-65)
   - Interest: Business growth, automation
   - Behavior: Follow business management pages
   - Income: $75K+

4. **Minimizers & Declutterers** (Age 25-55)
   - Interest: Decluttering, organizing, selling items
   - Behavior: Follow KonMari, minimalism content
   - Income: Varies

5. **E-commerce Sellers** (Age 25-50)
   - Interest: Shopify, Amazon, online selling
   - Behavior: Follow e-commerce pages
   - Income: $50K+

6. **Mobile-First Users** (Age 18-35)
   - Interest: Apps, tech, making money
   - Behavior: Active on social media
   - Income: $25K+

### Ad Placements

- Facebook Feed (60% budget)
- Instagram Feed (20% budget)
- Instagram Stories (10% budget)
- Instagram Reels (10% budget)

### Performance Goals

- CTR: 2-4%
- CPC: $0.50-$1.50
- Cost per Install: $0.50-$2.00
- ROAS: 3:1 or higher

## Ad Performance Tracking

### Metrics to Monitor

- **Impressions** - How many people see the ad
- **Clicks** - How many people click
- **CTR** (Click-Through Rate) - Clicks / Impressions
- **CPC** (Cost Per Click) - Total spend / Clicks
- **Conversions** - App installs, signups
- **CPA** (Cost Per Action) - Total spend / Conversions
- **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend) - Revenue / Ad spend
- **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares

### Weekly Reviews

- Check top performers
- Pause underperformers
- Increase budget for winners
- A/B test variations
- Adjust targeting if needed
