## Best Practices for Reels

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)
- Ask a question
- Show a problem
- Start with your result
- Use trending audio immediately
- Text overlay that stops thumb scroll

### Content (Middle)
- Keep pacing fast
- Show, don't tell
- Use text overlays for clarity
- Trending transitions
- Keep it vertical (9:16)

### CTA (Last 3-5 Seconds)
- Clear call to action
- "Download Free"
- "Link in bio"
- Direct URL if allowed
- Make it easy

### Audio Strategy
- Use 60-80% trending audio
- Keep dialogue brief
- Add sound effects for emphasis
- Match audio energy to message
- Consider captions (no sound viewers)

### Trending Topics to Leverage
- Side hustle season
- "Glow up" aesthetic
- Money-making trends
- Minimalism/decluttering
- Small business inspiration
- Time-saving hacks
- Before/after transformations

## Publishing Schedule

### Optimal Posting Times
- **Weekdays**: 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM
- **Weekends**: 9 AM, 12 PM, 8 PM
- **Test different times** and track performance

### Frequency
- **Instagram Reels**: 3-4 per week

### Content Mix
- 40% Educational (how-to, tutorials)
- 30% Inspirational (success stories, transformations)
- 20% Entertainment (challenges, funny, relatable)
- 10% Direct sales (app ads, offers)

## Analytics to Track

- **Views**: Total video views
- **Reach**: Unique accounts reached
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares
- **Profile Visits**: Clicks to profile
- **Follows**: New followers from video
- **Shares**: Video shares (indicates virality)
- **Saves**: Video saves (strong engagement indicator)
- **CTR**: Click-through rate to link in bio

### Target Performance
- **Views**: 1,000-10,000 per video (growing)
- **Engagement Rate**: 5%+
- **Save Rate**: 2%+
- **Share Rate**: 1%+
- **CPA**: $0.50-$2.00 (from video to install)

## Production Tips

### Filming
- Use natural light (window light best)
- Shoot in vertical format (9:16)
- Steady camera (use tripod or stabilizer)
- Clean background (decluttered)
- Good audio (quiet environment)

### Editing
- Keep it fast-paced
- Use trending transitions
- Add text overlays for clarity
- Include captions (CC)
- Proper color grading
- Consider Veed AI auto-edit features

### Thumbnails
- Clear, bold text
- Expressive face/emotion
- Brand colors (blue, gold, red)
- Call out the benefit
- Readable at small size
