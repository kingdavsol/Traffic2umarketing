## Production Guidelines

### Facebook Ad Image Specifications

**Image Size**: 1200 x 628 pixels (landscape)
**File Format**: PNG or JPG
**File Size**: < 5MB
**Resolution**: 72 DPI minimum
**Color Space**: RGB (sRGB)
**Safe Zone**: Keep content within 960 x 504 pixels (no text near edges)

**Text Guidelines**:
- Max 20% of image should be text
- High contrast (4.5:1 minimum)
- 14px minimum font size for legibility
- Bold fonts (700+) for visibility

### Instagram Story Ads

**Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 pixels (vertical)
**File Format**: PNG or JPG
**File Size**: < 8MB
**Aspect Ratio**: 9:16
**Safe Zone**: Avoid content within 50px of edges

### Video Production

**Format**: MP4 or MOV
**Resolution**: 1080 x 1920 (vertical) or 1920 x 1080 (horizontal)
**Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
**Bitrate**: 5-8 Mbps (quality) or 2-4 Mbps (mobile)
**Codec**: H.264 video, AAC audio
**Duration**: 15-60 seconds
**File Size**: < 50MB

**Revision Process**:
1. Designer submits design
2. Marketing lead reviews (1 day)
3. Feedback collected and compiled
4. Designer makes revisions (2 days)
5. Final approval and export (1 day)

**Common Revision Requests**:
- Adjust monster expression
- Modify color scheme
- Reposition text
- Change marketplace selection
- Adjust sizing/spacing
- Improve contrast
