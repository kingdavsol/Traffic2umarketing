================================================================================
                            CONCLUSION
================================================================================

POSTPARTUM FITNESS has the strongest MVP foundation - it's the only app with
substantive backend implementation. It should be the focus for rapid launch.

ADHD MANAGEMENT has the highest growth potential due to large market, but
needs body doubling and task AI which are more complex to implement.

INTERVIEW PREP has the strongest monetization ($12.99/mo) but requires
significant video infrastructure development.

All apps are fundamentally missing:
- Database and authentication
- User data persistence  
- Core feature implementations (despite landing pages claiming them)
- Payment processing
- Notification systems

Recommend phased approach:
1. Fix critical technical gaps (auth, DB) across all apps (2 weeks)
2. Prioritize PostPartum Fitness for MVP launch (2-3 weeks additional)
3. Parallelize other apps while learning from PPF launch
4. Focus on network effects for Local Services (requires different strategy)
