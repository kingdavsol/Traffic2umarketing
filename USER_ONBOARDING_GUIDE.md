## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Create Your Account (2 minutes)

1. **Go to**: https://quicksell.monster
2. **Click** "Sign Up"
3. **Enter**:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Password
4. **Click** "Create Account"
5. ✅ You're in!

---

### Step 2: Create Your First Listing (3 minutes)

#### Option A: Take a Photo (Recommended)

1. **Click** the "Create Listing" button
2. **Click** "Take Photo"
3. **Position** your item in the camera view
4. **Click** "Capture Photo"
5. ⏳ **Wait** 10 seconds while AI analyzes...
6. ✅ **Review** the AI-generated listing:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price
   - Condition

#### Option B: Upload a Photo

1. **Click** "Create Listing"
2. **Click** "Upload Photos"
3. **Select** photo(s) from your device
4. ⏳ **Wait** for AI analysis
5. ✅ **Review** the listing

#### Edit the Listing (Optional)

- **Title**: Edit if needed
- **Description**: AI writes professional descriptions!
- **Price**: Adjust based on AI suggestion
- **Category**: Change if incorrect
- **Condition**: Select: New, Like New, Good, Fair, Poor

---

### Step 3: Select Marketplaces (1 minute)

**Choose where to post:**

#### 🤖 Automated (Click and Done!)
- ✅ **Craigslist** - Posts automatically

#### ✋ Manual Copy/Paste (30 seconds each)
- ✅ **Facebook Marketplace** - 1-click copy
- ✅ **Mercari** - 1-click copy
- ⚠️ **OfferUp** - Requires mobile app

**Pro Tip**: Select all 4 marketplaces to maximize exposure!

---

### Step 4: Publish Your Listing

**Click** "Create Listing" button

**What Happens Next:**

#### Craigslist (Automated) 🤖
- ✅ Posts automatically
- ⏳ May need email verification (check inbox)
- 📧 Click verification link in email
- ✅ Listing goes live!

#### Facebook Marketplace (Manual) ✋
You'll see:
```
📘 Facebook
[Open Facebook] button

TITLE                    [Copy]
Your listing title here

DESCRIPTION             [Copy]
AI-generated description...

PRICE                    [Copy]
$XX.XX

[Copy All Fields]  ← Click this!

✅ Quick Steps:
1. Click "Copy All Fields" above
2. Click "Open Facebook" button
3. Paste into marketplace form
4. Upload photos and submit!
```

**Action**:
1. Click "Copy All Fields"
2. Click "Open Facebook" (opens new tab)
3. Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
4. Upload your photos
5. Click "Post"
6. ✅ Done!

#### Mercari (Manual) ✋
Same process as Facebook:
1. Click "Copy All Fields"
2. Click "Open Mercari"
3. Paste and upload photos
4. ✅ List!

#### OfferUp (Mobile App) 📱
1. Click "Copy All Fields"
2. Open OfferUp mobile app
3. Tap "Sell" button
4. Paste your details
5. Upload photos
6. ✅ Post!

2. **Clean background** - Remove clutter

## Common Questions ❓

### "How long does AI analysis take?"
**Answer**: 5-10 seconds per photo

### "Can I edit AI-generated listings?"
**Answer**: Yes! Edit any field before publishing

### "Do I need to post manually to all marketplaces?"
**Answer**: No! Craigslist is automatic. Others take 30 seconds each with 1-click copy.

### "What if AI gets the category wrong?"
**Answer**: Simply select the correct category from dropdown

### "How do I change my listing after posting?"
**Answer**: Go to "My Listings" → Edit → Make changes → Save

### "Can I upload multiple photos?"
**Answer**: Yes! Upload up to 5 photos per listing

### "What happens to Craigslist verification emails?"
**Answer**: Check your email, click the verification link, and your listing goes live

### "Why does OfferUp require the mobile app?"
**Answer**: OfferUp doesn't allow web posting for regular users - only mobile app

### "Can I mark items as sold on multiple marketplaces?"
**Answer**: Use "Mark as Sold" in My Listings when item sells anywhere

## Troubleshooting 🔧

### My Listings Page Not Loading
1. **Hard refresh**: `Ctrl+Shift+R` or `Cmd+Shift+R`
2. **Clear cache**: Right-click refresh → "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
3. **Check console**: F12 → Console tab for errors

### AI Analysis Failed
1. **Retake photo** - Ensure good lighting
2. **Try different angle** - Show item clearly
3. **Upload instead** - Use photo upload option

### Craigslist Posting Failed
1. **Check email** - Verification may be required
2. **Click verification link** - In email from Craigslist
3. **Wait 5 minutes** - Then check if listing is live

### Facebook/Mercari Paste Not Working
1. **Click "Copy All Fields"** - Must click before pasting
2. **Use keyboard shortcut** - Ctrl+V or Cmd+V
3. **Try individual fields** - Copy title, description, price separately

### Photos Not Uploading
1. **Check file size** - Keep under 5MB per photo
2. **Use JPG/PNG format** - Other formats may not work
3. **Try one photo** - Then add more
