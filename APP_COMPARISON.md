## Niche-Specific Competitive Positioning

### PostPartum Fitness - STRONGEST MVP
- Only app with substantive backend API implementation
- Has 7 phase-specific workouts with detailed data
- Considerations for delivery type differences
- Highest implementation maturity: 50%
- **Recommendation**: Most ready to launch MVP; focus on form checking AI and health tracking

### ADHD Management - HIGHEST GROWTH POTENTIAL
- Largest addressable market (5% of adults)
- High willingness to pay in target demographic
- Already strong community builders (Goblin Tools model)
- **Recommendation**: Needs rapid MVP iteration; body doubling feature is critical differentiator

### Interview Prep - HIGHEST MONETIZATION
- Premium tier at $12.99/mo (highest of group)
- Tech workers have high income/willingness to pay
- Recurring usage pattern (job search cycle driven)
- **Recommendation**: Develop live peer interview matching rapidly; this is the core loop

### Gig Worker Finance - HIGHEST MARKET URGENCY
- Time-sensitive (tax deadlines drive usage)
- Recurring revenue from consistent users
- Critical pain point for 59M+ gig workers
- **Recommendation**: Focus on tax deadline calendar and quarterly payment tracking first

### Mental Health Pro - BEST DEFENSIBILITY
- High switching costs once users build habit
- Corporate B2B2C potential
- High LTV through subscriptions
- **Recommendation**: Pursue corporate partnerships to distribute; individual app is weaker

### Local Services - HIGHEST PLATFORM POTENTIAL
- Marketplace network effects
- Multiple revenue streams (commission, featured listings, lead gen)
- Most ambitious but highest ceiling
- **Recommendation**: Build critical mass on supply side (providers) first; demand follows
