## 🚀 How to Sign Up for Affiliate Programs

### Step 1: Verify You're Ready
- [ ] Have your website live and getting traffic
- [ ] Ready to drive leads to insurance companies
- [ ] Can track conversions and attribute sales

### Step 2: Prepare Application Materials
- [ ] Company name/DBA
- [ ] Website URL
- [ ] Traffic sources (SEO, paid, etc.)
- [ ] Monthly traffic estimate
- [ ] Conversion rate expectations

### Step 3: Sign Up Process (3 approaches)

**Approach A: Direct from Company (Recommended)**
- Visit company's affiliate/partner page
- Click "Join our program" or "Apply"
- Fill out application form
- Wait 3-7 days for approval
- Receive API credentials or affiliate links

**Approach B: Through Affiliate Networks**
1. Create account on CJ Affiliate, ShareASale, or Impact Radius
2. Search for insurance company program
3. Click "Join"
4. Company approves (same-day to 3 days)
5. Start promoting immediately

**Approach C: Direct Outreach (For Higher Commissions)**
- Find company's partnership email
- Send professional pitch: "I'm launching [site name] for [niche], generating X leads/month"
- Propose exclusive commission rates
- Negotiate 30% higher commissions

### Step 4: Integration
- Get affiliate links or API access
- Add companies to your comparison tables
- Track clicks and conversions
- Monitor commissions monthly

1. **Layer Multiple Programs**
   - List 5-8 companies per site
   - Users choose based on features, not just price
   - You earn from whichever they pick

2. **Exclusive Deals**
   - Contact high-commission companies
   - Negotiate higher rates for volume
   - Offer exclusive positioning on site

3. **Seasonal Targeting**
   - Wedding season: Spring/summer
   - New Year resolutions: January (life insurance)
   - Tax season: March (disability, business)

4. **Email List Monetization**
   - Build email subscribers alongside
   - Send monthly tips + affiliate links
   - Second revenue stream from same audience

   - High-value users may be businesses
   - Referral to enterprise sales teams
