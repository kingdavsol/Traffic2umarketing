❓ Objection Handling

Objection Answer
"Is Craigslist automation legal?" Yes! QuickSell uses browser automation (like a human clicking buttons). It's no different than auto-filling forms. We follow Craigslist's terms of service and rate limits.
"I don't trust AI to write my listings" You're in full control! Review and edit every field before posting. But most users find the AI is 90%+ accurate and just makes minor tweaks.
"I only sell on one marketplace" Perfect! QuickSell still saves you 15 minutes per listing with AI-powered creation. Plus, posting to multiple marketplaces increases your chances of selling faster.
"Is my data secure?" Yes. We use bank-level encryption for all credentials. We never store marketplace passwords in plain text. Your data is yours.
"What if I need help?" We offer support via email, live chat (Pro users), and comprehensive documentation. Plus, our onboarding guide walks you through everything.
